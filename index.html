Research

Research Interests

I am currently studying model-theoretic asymptotics, with a focus on zero-one laws and first-order limit behavior for classes of finite structures and models.

Model Theory

Fraisse classes, first-order limit properties, and logical structure in families of models.

Zero-One Phenomena

Asymptotic truth values of first-order sentences over natural classes of finite structures.

Asymptotic Behavior

Limit behavior of classes of models from logical, combinatorial, and algebraic viewpoints.
